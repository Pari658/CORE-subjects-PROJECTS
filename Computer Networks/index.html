How CDN Works

Without CDN

Requests go through multiple edge servers to reach the origin server, resulting in high latency due to longer network path.

With CDN

Content is cached on nearby CDN servers. Requests are served directly from the closest CDN node without reaching the origin.

Benefits

Reduced latency (60-80% faster), lower bandwidth costs, improved availability, and significantly better user experience.
